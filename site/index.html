Understanding the Dashboard

Growth Score:

A normalized score (0-100) representing predicted commercial growth potential. Higher scores indicate neighborhoods with stronger predicted growth signals based on permits, construction value, zoning, and business activity patterns.

Predicted Count:

The actual number of new business licences predicted for the next year (t+1) based on features from the current year (t).

Scenario Mode:

Run "what-if" analyses by adjusting key indicators. See how hypothetical changes to permits, construction value, or zoning would affect growth predictions and rankings.

Methodology

Growth Definition: Predicted number of new business licences in year (t+1) based on features from year t.

Features: Business activity, development permits, building permits, zoning capacity, and vibrancy indicators.

Model: Gradient Boosting (LightGBM) with time-based train/test split.

Validation: Trained on earlier years, tested on most recent 1-2 years.

Disclaimer: Explanations indicate correlation, not causation. This is a proxy measure.
