Methodology

Growth Definition: Predicted number of new business licences in year (t+1) based on features from year t.

Features: Business activity, development permits, building permits, zoning capacity, and vibrancy indicators.

Model: Gradient Boosting (LightGBM) with time-based train/test split.

Validation: Trained on earlier years, tested on most recent 1-2 years.

Limitations: This is a proxy measure. Actual growth depends on many factors not captured in open data.
